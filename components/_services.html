Some tips and tricks to help in the moving process

Tips

  • "Packing beforehand will speed up the moving process by up to 4x"
  • "Use new and sturdy boxes to maximize protection as heavier loaded boxes to tend to move and tear more"
  • "Pets should be kept apart from the move in order to maximze efficiency"
  • "If you can't lift the box, we probably can't either. Load boxes as if you were going to carry them."
